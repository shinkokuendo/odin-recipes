Chicken Curry Recipe

Filipino Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a chicken cooked in coconut milk with added curry powder to spice up the flavor. It is best served with rice. It is paired with the common veggies like potatoes, carrots and the bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of chicken
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 3 medium sized potatoes
  • 1 medim sized carrot
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • half head of garlic (minced)
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 thumb ginger
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 chicken knorr cube
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • a little bit of cooking oil
  1. Heat up your cooking pot. Once heated, pour a little bit of oil in it.
  2. Then, put the garlic. Wait it until brown.
  3. Add the chicken. Fry it until half cooked.
  4. Add a little bit of salt to add some flavor in the chicken.
  5. Add the knorr cube.
  6. Add the water and the coconut milk. Wait it until boiled.
  7. Add the veggies and the curry powder
  8. When it is almost done, add the bell pepper.
  9. And lastly, the salt and pepper depending on your taste.
