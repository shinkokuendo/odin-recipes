Menudo Recipe

Filipino Pork and Liver Menudo

These recipes with tomato sauce such as chicken afritada, menudo, and calderetta. They only varies on the meat that you included in your ingredients like chicken for afritada, pork for menudo and beef for calderetta. So, most of the ingredients are the same like the potatoes, carrots, bay leaves, tomato sauce, a thickener, a little bit of sugar, salt, and pepper. Others, add a little bit of raisins, hotdog and red and green bell pepper. So, the taste varies depending on the meat that that you included. For instance, this menudo, you have pork and liver. You can check out the ingredients below.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of pork
  • 1/4 kg of chicken liver (blanched it and separate from the water)
  • 1 tbsp of cornstarch mix with 1 tbsp of cold water
  • 1 medium size carrots
  • 3 fist size of potatoes
  • dice of red and green bell pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • half head of garlic
  • 1 red onion
  • 4-5 bay leaves
  • 1 pork knorr cubes
  • sugar, salt and pepper to taste
  • a little bit of cooking oil
  1. Prepare all the ingredients. Chopped and slice those that needs to be chopped.
  2. Heat up your cooking pot. Once heated, pour a little bit of oil in it.
  3. Then, put the garlic. Wait it until brown. Then, add the onion
  4. Add the pork. Fry it until half cooked.
  5. Then, Add the liver.
  6. Add the pork cubes and add a little bit of salt to add some flavor in the pork and liver.
  7. Add the tomato sauce and the water.
  8. Wait until boiled, then, add the potatoes and the carrots
  9. When it is almost done, add the rest of the ingredients.
  10. And lastly, the sugar, salt and pepper depending on your taste.
